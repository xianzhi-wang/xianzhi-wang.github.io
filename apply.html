PhD Applicants

  1. Application deadlines: 15 January 2020 (for commencemnt July 2020) or 30 June 2020 (for commencement January 2021).

  2. Register your account at eApplication website.

  3. Select Type of course (Doctor of Philosophy), Course code (C02029), Principle supervisor (Wang, Xianzhi), Faculty (Engineering and Information Technology), Do you want to be considered for UTS scholarships (Yes).

  4. Submit documents: your passport (bio page), CV/resume, Research proposal (2-3 pages), Academic transcripts, Education Certificates, English test results (IELTS: overall 6.5, Writing 6.0; or equivalent scores in other tests, e.g., TOFEL).

Master by Research Applicants

  1. Deadlines, procedure, and required documents are the same as PhD application.

  2. The only difference is that you need to select Type of course (Master by Research) during the online application.

Visiting Professor/Fellow/Scholar/Student Applicants

  1. Complete Honorary Appointment Form and fill in: your personal details (Section A), start/end dates, research proposal (Section D).

  2. Send me the completed form, your passport (bio page), resume, and valid Australian visa (if applicable) via E-mail.

  3. Apply for a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408) via ImmiAccount after receiving an official sposnorship letter from UTS HR.

Notes

PhD Applicants:

  • Please add a note in your application, indicating whether you are applying for CSC or joint/dual PhD degrees.

  • Existing PhD students of following univesities are eligible to apply for joint/dual degrees: Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Beijing Institute of Technology, Shanghai University, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Sun Yat-sen University, Southern University of Science and Technology.

PhD and Master Applicants:

  • English requirement might be waived if you have studied full-time for a least one year in English-speaking countries.

  • You may discuss your research proposal with me before submission.
