Apply for PhD

Duration: 3–3.5 years; stipend: AU$27,596 per annum; tuition fee waived; top-up scholarships will be provided to CSC students.

  1. Register your account at UTS Online Application System.

  2. Select Type of course (Doctor of Philosophy), Course code (C02029), Principle supervisor (Wang, Xianzhi), Faculty (Engineering and Information Technology), Do you want to be considered for UTS scholarships (Yes).

  3. Submit your passport bio page, CV/resume, Research proposal (2-3 pages), Academic transcripts, Education Certificates, English test results. You might be waived of English language requirements (IELTS Academic: 6.5 overall, writing 6.0, or equivalent scores in other tests) if you have studied full-time for a least one year in English-speaking countries.

  4. Put a few words in your application, indicating whether you are applying for CSC-UTS PhD Scholarship or Dual/Joint-Degree Programs or neither.

  5. Submit your application before deadlines: 15 January 2020 (International) or 30 April 2020 (Domestic).

Apply for Master by Research

Duration: 1.5–2 years. All Master students are supposed to be self-funded.

  1. Follow the same procedure, meet the same deadlines and English language requirements, and submit the same documents as PhD applicants.

  2. Only one difference: select Type of course (Master by Research) during your online application.

Visiting Professor/Fellow/Scholar/Student

UTS Rankings in Computer Science: World (QS: 51-100, Times: 74, USNews: 39, ARWU: 29); Domestic (QS: 4-7, Times: 2, USNews: 2, ARWU: 1)

  1. Complete Honorary Appointment Form by putting in your personal details (Section A), start/end dates, research proposal (Section D).

  2. E-mail me the completed form, your passport bio page, resume, and valid Australian visa (if applicable).

  3. Apply for CSC-UTS Visitor Scholarship or other support (if applicable), using the invitation/sponsorship letter from UTS.

  4. Apply for a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408) using the invitation/sposnorship letter from UTS (if you need a visa).
