Apply for PhD

Eligibility: Applicant must hold or will hold a Master's or Bachelor (honors)'s degree.
Duration: 3–3.5 years; Stipend: AU$27,596 per annum (tax free); Tuition fee: Waived; Top-ups: Will be provided to CSC students.

  1. Register your account at UTS Online Application System.

  2. Select Type of course: Doctor of Philosophy; Course code: C02029; Principle supervisor: Wang, Xianzhi; Faculty: Engineering and Information Technology; Be considered for UTS scholarships: Yes.

  3. Submit your passport bio page, CV, Research proposal (2-3 pages), Academic transcripts, Education certificates, English test resultsIELTS Academic (6.5 overall, writing 6.0) or equivalent other scores; Waivers may apply..

  4. Put a few words in your application, indicating whether you are applying for CSC-UTS PhD Scholarship or Dual/Joint-Degree ProgramsAccessible to early-stage PhD students from KTP Universities. or neither.

  5. Submit your application before deadlines: 15 January 2020 (International) or 30 April 2020 (Domestic).

  2. Then, follow the same procedure (excluding Step 4) and deadlines, and submit the same documents as required for PhD applicants.

Visiting Professor/Fellow/Scholar/Student

UTS Rankings in Computer Science: World (QS: 51-100, Times: 74, USNews: 39, ARWU: 29); Domestic (QS: 4-7, Times: 2, USNews: 2, ARWU: 1)

  1. Complete Honorary Appointment Form by putting in your personal details (Section A), start/end dates, research proposal (Section D).

  2. Send me the completed form, your passport bio page, CV, and valid Australian visa (if applicable) via E-mail.

  3. Apply for CSC-UTS Visitor Scholarship or other supporte.g., KTP Visiting Fellow Program (if applicable), using the invitation/sponsorship letter from UTS.

  4. Apply for a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408)Required documents: sponsorship letter, Health Insurance Certificate (OVHC), Health Certificate, No Criminal Record Certificate, Financial Support Certificate. using the invitation/sposnorship letter from UTS (if you need a visa).
