Research Degree Applicants

Deadlines: 15 January 2020 (for commencemnt July 2020) or 30 June 2020 (for commencement January 2021).

PhD Applicants

Duration: 3–3.5 years; stipend: AU$27,596 per annum; top-up scholarships will be provided to CSC students.

  1. Register your account and fill in your personal details at eApplication.

  2. Select Type of course (Doctor of Philosophy), Course code (C02029), Principle supervisor (Wang, Xianzhi), Faculty (Engineering and Information Technology), Do you want to be considered for UTS scholarships (Yes).

  3. Submit documents: your passport (bio page), CV/resume, Research proposal (2-3 pages), Academic transcripts, Education Certificates, English test results* (IELTS: overall 6.5, Writing 6.0; or equivalent scores in other tests, e.g., TOFEL).

  4. Put a few words in your application, indicating whether you are applying for CSC or joint/dual degrees^.

Master by Research Applicants

Duration: 1.5–2 years.

  1. Follow the same deadlines, procedure, and required documents as PhD applicants.

  2. Only one difference: select Type of course (Master by Research) during your online application.

* You might be waived of English requirements if you have studied full-time for a least one year in English-speaking countries.

^ Existing PhD students from the following univesities might be eligible to apply for joint/dual PhD degrees: Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Beijing Institute of Technology, Shanghai University, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Sun Yat-sen University, Southern University of Science and Technology.

Visiting Professor/Fellow/Scholar/Student Applicants

  1. Complete Honorary Appointment Form and fill in: your personal details (Section A), start/end dates, research proposal (Section D).

  2. Send me the completed form, your passport (bio page), resume, and valid Australian visa (if applicable) via E-mail.

  3. Apply for a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408) via ImmiAccount after receiving an official sposnorship letter from UTS HR.
