Apply for PhD

Duration: 3–3.5 years; stipend: AU$27,596 per annum; tuition fee waiver; top-up scholarships will be provided to CSC students.

  1. Register your account at UTS Online Application System.

  2. Select Type of course (Doctor of Philosophy), Course code (C02029), Principle supervisor (Wang, Xianzhi), Faculty (Engineering and Information Technology), Do you want to be considered for UTS scholarships (Yes).

  3. Submit your passport (bio page), CV/resume, Research proposal (2-3 pages), Academic transcripts, Education Certificates, English test results.

  4. You might be waived of English language requirements (IELTS Academic: 6.5 overall, writing 6.0, or equivalent scores in other tests) if you have studied full-time for a least one year in English-speaking countries.

  5. Put a few words in your application, indicating whether you are applying for CSC or joint/dual degrees.

  6. Submit your application before deadlines: 15 January 2020 (for commencemnt July 2020) or 30 June 2020 (for commencement January 2021).

Apply for Master by Research

Duration: 1.5–2 years. All Master students are supposed to be self-funded.

  1. Follow the same deadlines, procedure, and required documents as PhD applicants.

  2. Only one difference: select Type of course (Master by Research) during your online application.

Visiting Professor/Fellow/Scholar/Student

UTS Rankings in Computer Science: World (QS: 51-100, Times: 74, USNews: 39, ARWU: 29); Domestic (QS: 4-7, Times: 2, USNews: 2, ARWU: 1)

  1. Complete Honorary Appointment Form and fill in: your personal details (Section A), start/end dates, research proposal (Section D).

  2. Send me the completed form, your passport (bio page), resume, and valid Australian visa (if applicable) via E-mail.

  3. If you require a visa, apply for a Temporary Activity visa (subclass 408) via ImmiAccount after receiving an official sposnorship letter from UTS HR.
