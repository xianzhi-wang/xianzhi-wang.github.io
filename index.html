About me

I received the B.E. degree in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong Univeristy in 2007, and the M.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2009 and 2014, respectively.

Before joining UTS, I worked at University of Adelaide, University of New South Wales, and Singapore Management University. I was previously a Visiting Scholar at Arizona State University and Research Intern at IBM Research - China. I am the recipent of the Australian Research Council Discovery Early Career Research Award (DECRA) in 2017, IBM PhD Fellowship in 2013, and CSC Scholarship in 2010.

Research

My research interests include: Internet of Things, Data Fusion, Machine Learning, Recommendation, Cybersecurity.
