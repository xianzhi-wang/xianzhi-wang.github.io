About me

I received the B.E. degree in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong Univeristy in 2007, and the M.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2009 and 2014. Before joining UTS, I worked at University of Adelaide, University of New South Wales, and Singapore Management University, respectively. I was a Visiting Scholar at Arizona State University and Research Intern at IBM Research - China.

I am the recipent of Australian Research Council Discovery Early Career Researcher Award (DECRA) 2017, IBM PhD Fellowship 2013, National Scholarship for Doctoral Students 2012, IBM-CSC China Excellent Student Scholarship 2012, Best Paper Award of 1st CCF National Conference on Service Computing (NCSC) 2010, CSC Scholarship 2010, and Excellent Postgraduate Award (Gold Prize) of Harbin Institute of Technology 2009.

Research

My research interests include: Internet of Things, Data Fusion, Machine Learning, Recommendation, Cybersecurity.
